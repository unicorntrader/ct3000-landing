BEHAVIOR
⚠ 3 revenge trades opened within an hour of a loss. ˅
WHY FLAGGED
Revenge trading is entering an off-plan trade right after a loss — often sized larger — to "get it back". It's emotion-driven, not edge-driven.
WHAT TO TRY
Set a hard rule: after any losing trade, no new entry for 30 minutes. If the idea still looks good after the cool-off, it was probably real.
